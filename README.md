# SignalX

### AI Early Warning System for Student Success

**Team Void | HackIndia 2026**

---

## 🚀 Overview

SignalX is an AI-powered student risk intelligence platform designed to help educational institutions proactively identify and address student challenges before they become large-scale crises.

Using Generative AI and predictive analytics, SignalX analyzes student discussions, feedback, and community signals to detect early warning signs related to:

* Placement Anxiety
* Burnout Risk
* Internship Scarcity
* Skill Gaps
* Cyber Scam Exposure

The platform transforms raw student conversations into actionable intelligence, enabling institutions to make data-driven decisions and provide timely interventions.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Educational institutions often identify student issues only after they have already impacted academic performance, placements, mental well-being, or career growth.

Current approaches are reactive rather than proactive.

Institutions lack tools to:

* Detect emerging student concerns
* Predict future challenges
* Monitor risk trends at scale
* Generate actionable recommendations

As a result, many issues remain unnoticed until they become major problems.

---

## 💡 Solution

SignalX acts as an AI-powered Early Warning System for Student Success.

The platform:

1. Analyzes student discussions and feedback.
2. Detects hidden risk signals using AI.
3. Predicts future student challenges.
4. Generates strategic recommendations.
5. Visualizes risk hotspots through dashboards and heatmaps.

This enables institutions to intervene early and improve student outcomes.

## 🏗 System Workflow

1. Student discussions are submitted for analysis.
2. Gemini AI processes the text.
3. SignalX identifies risk indicators.
4. Risk scores are generated.
5. Forecasts and recommendations are created.
6. Insights are displayed through dashboards and heatmaps.
